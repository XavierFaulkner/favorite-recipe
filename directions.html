Spaghetti with ground beef Recipe

Home Ingredients Directions

Step-by-Step Directions

  1. Boil pasta in a large pot with salted water, as much as you prefer.
  2. While pasta is boiling, add olive oil to a pan and set heat to medium/high.
  3. Once the pan is hot, add onions and cook until they are a bit transparent.
  4. Add ground beef to the pan mixing it in with the onions, cook until all the meat is brown.
  5. Check to see if your pasta is done. If so, remove the pot from the heat and drain the pasta.
  6. When all ground beef has turned brown, reduce the heat to medium and add garlic, salt, pepper, and any other seasoning of your choice.
  7. Cook the meat for an additional 6-8 minutes stiring often.
  8. Add the tomato sauce and cook for an additional 5-6 minutes.
  9. Serve the pasta with the sauce and enjoy!
